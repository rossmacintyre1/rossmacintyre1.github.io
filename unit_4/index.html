We're increasingly digital

I’m deeply fascinated in how the on and offline environments have irreversibly collapsed into one, and technology is increasingly integrating into our everyday lives. Users interchangeably move from the real to the virtual world without hesitation, seamlessly. Companies are recognising this, however it is simply the pace at which people are shifting their behaviours and attitudes that are resulting in companies being left behind. New devices, software updates, another social media platform, it is a constant macro demand to provide more convenience, speed and control, and users will gravitate towards the brands that deliver in these areas. It's time to keep up with change.
